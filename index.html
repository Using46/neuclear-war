Understanding the physics

You might be wondering how every major city in the US got replaced with a smoldering crater because of an atom? Well thats because of nuclear fission!... Or fusion!... Depending on the bomb! At this point the detials probably don't matter to you so lets get down to business.

An unstable atom splits into two or more smaller pieces that are more stable, and releases energy (gamma rays) in the process. This energy can then split additional atoms and cause a nuclear fission chain reaction which leads up to a massive nuclear explosion.

A nuclear explosion swallowing an island

Radiation is energy. This can be in the form of a wave or particle. Not all radiation is directly harmful. Radiation can span from radio waves used for phones, to gamma rays which blow apart your DNA. Radiation, energy, frequencies, waves, and vibrations are all the same thing. Radiation is energy, energy is waves, waves are vibrations, and vibrations are oscillating frequencies.

Some of the molecules in your body are polar, meaning they have a positive and negative side (think of a compass). These molecules orient themselves with whichever magnetic field they are in (like a compass). An electromagnetic wave oscillates (goes up and down) its magnetic field. This causes the polar molecules to spin back and forth. When a molecule is flipping up and down over 10,000,000,000,000,000,000 times a second… It starts to create problems...
